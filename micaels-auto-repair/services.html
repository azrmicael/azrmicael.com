Our Services

Micael's Auto Repair offers quality auto repair services you can count on. Our experienced team can get you fixed and back on the road in no time.

Our technicians service all makes and models of cars, trucks and motorcycles. We do everything from routine maintenance to engine and transmission replacements, fuel system repairs and more. We use the best parts in the industry to fix your car or truck for the first time, so you can stay on the road and not in the shop.

Our Auto Repair Services Include:

Engine service
Oil change
Tune Ups
Scheduled maintenance
Computer diagnostics
Cabin filters
Brake service
Air conditioning service
Wheel balancing
General repairs
Full service tire center
Performance work

In addition to our other services we offer towing in case your vehicle is not able to make it into the shop.
