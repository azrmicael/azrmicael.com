About Micael's Auto Repair

Caring for vehicles since 1988.

Until Here

Since we opened our door in 1987, Micael's Auto Repair has been the destination for full service auto repair in Fresno California. We are proud to be an independent, Family owned auto repair facility that focuses on the needs of our customers, and define for ourselves what it means to achieve success.

We have built our reputation on giving the best car advice possible, and give our customers confidence in our guidance with our knowledge and expertise. At the end of the day, we want our customers to walk away knowing that they can trust us. We care about them as an individual, not just a number.

Visit Accelerate Automotive, and you'll see our team with big smiles on their faces. We're passionate about what we do. It's why we're so good at our job, and why our customers are so loyal. And, like our co-founder Micael says, "When you're passionate about what you do for a living it really shows in the way that you deliver. I can honestly say that our delivery is second to none, and that's the reason why I love this job, I love this place and I love the customers that come here - simple as that!"
